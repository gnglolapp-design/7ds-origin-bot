Meliodas

Darkness

Axe

Darkness
Passive

Speedmaster

Increases damage dealt with Normal Skill by 20% for 20 sec when using a Normal Skill. Increases damage dealt by 100% at 3 stacks, then resets.

Normal Attack

Normal Attack

Inflicts damage equal to 146% of Attack. 1st: 22% 2nd: 24% 3rd: 38% 4th: 62%

Special Attack

Special Attack

12 sec cooldown. Inflicts damage equal to 148–200% of Attack based on charge level. Inflicts Demon Energy for 10 sec. Demon Energy: inflicts damage equal to 200% of ATK when the effect is removed.

Normal Skill

Circle Slash

Teleports behind an enemy affected by Demon Energy within 30m and inflicts damage equal to 245% of Attack. Attacking Demon Energy resets Normal Skill cooldown and removes Demon Energy.

Attack Skill

Power Arc

Inflicts damage equal to 123% of Attack.

Ultimate Move

Demon's Fighting Spirit

10 sec cooldown. Consumes 4 magic. Inflicts damage equal to 370% of Attack and inflicts Demon Energy for 10 sec. If 5 or fewer targets: damage increases by up to 100% (1 enemy: +100%, 2: +50%, 3: +33%, 4: +25%, 5: +20%).
